CALI

Enjoy your time in Colombia's most vibrant and happy city!

Top three activities to do in Cali, Colombia.

"PARQUE DEL GATO" CAT PARK

A park with one large cat sculpture and 15 smaller ones, all painted by different Colombian artists.

"CARNAVAL DE CALI" CALI'S FESTIVAL

The Cali Fair, held every year from the 25th to the 30th of December, is one of the most important traditional celebrations in the country and on the continent. Events such as bullfights, beauty pageants, fashion shows, concerts by some of the most famous musicians in the world, and, of course, salsa festivals are held during the Fair.

CHOLADO

The cholado, that kind of magical fusion of colors and flavors, is one of the typical products of Cali, especially if one is hot and thirsty. If you are visiting Cali, you must not miss this legendary sweet appetizer.
